Chocolate strawberries

Ingredients:
Water (1 cup)
Strawberries (however many you want)
Milk/Dark/White Chocolate (however much you want)

1)Boil water
2)Break chocolate into pieces and put in a saucepan
3)Place the saucepan in the boiling water until chocolate is melted
4)Place strawberries on a sheet of wax paper and dip or drizzle chocolate over them
